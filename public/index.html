• Ild i Gilden

    I have since 2025 updated and maintained Ild i Gildens website.

    Ild i Gilden is a danish folk festival a bit outside Roskilde, i attend every year and cannot recommend enouth.

    Their website has a really lovly charm and design that i cannot take credit for myself. The static site is hosted on heztner where diffrent html fragments are manualy changed and pasted together by Gupl-js.

  • Custom departure board

    A dynamic display board that shows departure times for public transport.

    This is a small project born out of frustrations of cheking every departure board individually for the differnet bus stops around my appartment. I developed into a small concise list of departures around me I could check from my phone before leaving my appartment.

    It is a dynamicly rendered website that i hosted localy on my network. It uses the rejseplanen api, and is comfigured using a json file.
